---

## Methodology

Every student's name was matched against every 2026 bank deposit across all institutional accounts. Only cases where the **bank total exceeds the database/DKM total paid by more than K500** are listed.

**Filters applied to eliminate false positives:**
- 2025 deposits excluded (prior period — benefit of doubt)
- Both first AND last name must appear in the depositor field (no partial matches)
- Common surnames appearing for multiple students: cross-checked to ensure no name collision
- MIHAS Account 592 excluded (indexing/other fees — separate category)
- KATC Account 832 excluded (Gratuity — separate fee not tracked in tuition summary)
- Threshold: only discrepancies > K500 reported

**What this means:** These are cases where a student deposited money into the institution's bank account in their own name in 2026, and that money does NOT appear in their student fee record. The money is confirmed in the bank — it was received but not posted.

## CONCLUSION

**K253,375** in confirmed bank deposits across 91 students were received by the institutions but not recorded in student fee records. This analysis uses only 2026 data, requires exact name matches, and excludes all ambiguous cases.

The concentration in specific sections (COG 7, EHT 6, EHT 5) suggests the issue may be linked to specific individuals responsible for recording payments in those sections rather than a system-wide random error.
