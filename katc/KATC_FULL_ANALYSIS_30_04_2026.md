## 5. KEY FINDINGS & RECOMMENDATIONS

### Finding 1: K53,150 in Unrecorded Payments (CRITICAL)
12 out of 14 genuinely unpaid students have confirmed bank deposits in their exact names that are NOT recorded in the student database. This represents a **recording/reconciliation failure**, not a payment failure.

**Action Required:** Immediately reconcile these 12 students' records. Update the database to reflect the bank-confirmed payments.

### Finding 2: Bad Debts May Be Overstated by K10,800+
4 students on the Bad Debts list have confirmed bank deposits. Salome Nkwambana alone has K5,100 in confirmed deposits.

**Action Required:** Review bad debt classifications against bank records before write-off.

### Finding 3: Mwila Lukwesa Appears in Both Zero-Payment AND Bad Debts
This student appears on the RN-6 active roster (K0 paid, K8,200 balance) AND on the Bad Debts sheet. The bank shows a K200 deposit in January 2026.

**Action Required:** Clarify this student's status — are they active or written off?

### Finding 4: Two Students Have K0 Fees (Anomalous)
Rosemary Musheneti and Jordan Banda show K0 total fees, K0 paid, K0 balance. Yet Rosemary has a K4,200 bank deposit from October 2025. These may be students who completed their program but the fees column was cleared.

**Action Required:** Verify whether these students have graduated or if their fee records were accidentally cleared.

### Finding 5: No Computer Numbers for Some Students
Jessy Saminganja and Vasi Sakala (RN-5) have no computer numbers assigned. This makes tracking and reconciliation harder.

**Action Required:** Assign computer numbers to all active students.

### Finding 6: Deposit Naming Convention
All bank deposits follow the pattern "POS Cardless Deposit-Agent [agent_id] [agent_name] [location] [reference] [depositor_name]". The depositor name at the end is the primary identifier. Some deposits include explicit notes like "TUITION FEE-VASI SAKALA" which makes reconciliation straightforward.

**Recommendation:** Require students to include their computer number in the deposit reference to enable automated reconciliation.
